     9вҶ’     9вҶ’## 0. EXECUTIVE SUMMARY
    10вҶ’    10вҶ’
    11вҶ’    11вҶ’**Project Noor** is a 2D top-down pixel RPG that plays like a PokГ©mon-generation handheld game (Gen 3вҖ“5 feel, Coromon/Nexomon-adjacent UX) but replaces monster-catching/battling with **witnessing and documenting Philippine history**. The player is a 2026 college student pulled through a mysterious letter into San Diego, 1887 вҖ” the fictional town of *Noli Me Tangere* вҖ” and later into the Manila of *El Filibusterismo*, 1891. They cannot change what happens to Ibarra, Elias, Sisa, Maria Clara, or Simoun. They can only explore, ask questions, solve puzzles, and record what they witness in a **Rizal Codex** вҖ” becoming, by the end, an *Illustrado*: someone who truly understands.
    12вҶ’    12вҶ’
    13вҶ’    13вҶ’- **Engine:** Phaser 3 (game canvas) inside **React + TypeScript** (shell/UI/menus)
    14вҶ’    14вҶ’- **Backend:** Node.js + Express, **PostgreSQL**, **JWT** auth
    15вҶ’    15вҶ’- **Platforms:** Web (primary), Desktop wrapper later (Tauri/Electron), Mobile after that
    16вҶ’    16вҶ’- **Tone target:** All-ages, roughly a "T for Teen" equivalent вҖ” the novels contain death, abuse, and sexual menace; Section 21 below defines exactly how much of that is shown vs. narrated.

    20вҶ’    20вҶ’## 1. VISION STATEMENT
    21вҶ’    21вҶ’
    22вҶ’    22вҶ’> *"Noli Me Tangere and El Filibusterismo are usually assigned as homework. Project Noor is designed so that a 15-year-old finishes it having voluntarily reread Rizal's footnotes вҖ” not because they were tested, but because they were there."*
    23вҶ’    23вҶ’
    24вҶ’    24вҶ’The game must never feel like edutainment filler bolted onto a quiz. Every mechanic borrowed from PokГ©mon exists because it already teaches a habit the game needs (curiosity about hidden areas, talking to every NPC, completing a "dex," caring about a badge/medal count). We are reusing a proven **behavioral loop**, not just an aesthetic.

    28вҶ’    28вҶ’## 2. GAME PILLARS
    29вҶ’    29вҶ’
    30вҶ’    30вҶ’1. **Witness, Don't Rewrite** вҖ” Player agency is exploratory and interpretive, never plot-altering. Side quests can help minor/invented NPCs; they never save Elias, un-imprison Sisa, or stop the execution. This is a design pillar, not a limitation вҖ” the tragedy landing on schedule *is* the theme (see Padre Florentino's thesis in Section 21).
    31вҶ’    31вҶ’2. **Curiosity Is the Currency** вҖ” Replace "grinding EXP off wild encounters" with "grinding Knowledge XP off curiosity" (reading signs, opening books, asking a fourth follow-up question).
    32вҶ’    32вҶ’3. **The Codex Is the Real Endgame** вҖ” Like a PokГ©dex completion run, 100%-ing the Rizal Codex is the long-tail goal for replay-minded players.
    33вҶ’    33вҶ’4. **Two Novels, One Emotional Arc** вҖ” Noli's hope must curdle into Fili's cynicism *mechanically*, not just narratively (see Section 9, tone-shift techniques).
    34вҶ’    34вҶ’5. **Respectful of the Source** вҖ” Historical figures (Rizal himself) are never playable, never given invented dialogue that contradicts the historical record, and never trivialized. Fictional characters (Ibarra, Simoun, Elias, etc.) have full creative latitude since they're Rizal's own inventions.

   245вҶ’   245вҶ’## 9. STORY DESIGN вҖ” TONE-SHIFT TECHNIQUE (Noli вҶ’ Fili)
   246вҶ’   246вҶ’
   247вҶ’   247вҶ’Mechanically encode the novels' own thematic pivot (reform вҶ’ accelerationism):
   248вҶ’   248вҶ’- **Palette:** Noli areas are warm/golden (hope); Fili areas desaturate, more blue/grey, harsher shadows.
   249вҶ’   249вҶ’- **Music:** Noli's main motif is major-key kundiman-inspired; the *same* motif returns in Fili transposed to minor key.
   250вҶ’   250вҶ’- **UI chrome:** Journal paper texture ages/yellows between arcs.
   251вҶ’   251вҶ’- **Mini-game framing:** Noli puzzles are framed as "help/build" (assemble the school cornerstone plaque); Fili puzzles are framed as "uncover/expose" (assemble the truth behind a bombing plot).
   252вҶ’   252вҶ’- **NPC callback system:** Every named Fili-arc NPC who appeared as a background sprite in Noli (a student who is now Isagani's classmate, a merchant who is now Quiroga's clerk) should be flagged in the Codex as a "Then and Now" pair вҖ” a very PokГ©mon-esque "you caught this one before it evolved" dopamine hit, done here through recognition rather than stat growth.

   302вҶ’   302вҶ’## 11. DIALOGUE SYSTEM
   303вҶ’   303вҶ’
   304вҶ’   304вҶ’- **Branching but non-lethal to progress:** every branch is flavor/characterization, never a fail-state. This keeps the "witness, don't rewrite" pillar intact while still giving PokГ©mon-style dialogue-choice satisfaction.
   305вҶ’   305вҶ’- **Two dialogue registers, togglable in Settings:** (a) Filipino-flavored English (period-appropriate but readable вҖ” "Salamat, iho, but I must speak with your father first"), and (b) a Tagalog-primary mode for classroom use. Never machine-translate; write both natively.
   306вҶ’   306вҶ’- **Speech bubble rules borrowed from PokГ©mon:** portrait bust on one side, name plate, A-to-advance, hold-to-fast-forward, auto-log every line to the Journal.
   307вҶ’   307вҶ’- **Historical figures' dialogue is paraphrase-bounded:** no invented dialogue is ever presented as a *direct quote* from Rizal's actual biography (only the novel's fictional characters get full invented dialogue, since they're already fictional).

   443вҶ’   443вҶ’## 21. HISTORICAL ACCURACY & CONTENT-SENSITIVITY NOTES
   444вҶ’   444вҶ’
   445вҶ’   445вҶ’The source novels contain domestic abuse, wrongful imprisonment, torture, exhumation, a child's beating, a mother's descent into madness, execution, attempted/implied sexual assault (Padre Camorra toward Juli), and suicide (Juli's death, Maria Clara's threatened death). Given the likely player age range (Section 3 includes Grade 9вҖ“10 students), the build should:
   446вҶ’   446вҶ’
   447вҶ’   447вҶ’- **Depict consequences, not mechanics.** Sisa's madness, Juli's death, and Elias's death are shown through **aftermath, framing, and dialogue**, never through graphic on-screen depiction of the act itself. Use screen-fades, off-screen sound design, and NPC reaction shots вҖ” the same technique the *novel itself* often uses (Rizal narrates around the worst moments too).
   448вҶ’   448вҶ’- **No interactive self-harm or violence mechanics.** These sequences are cutscene-only; the player never presses a button that "does" the harmful act.
   449вҶ’   449вҶ’- **Padre Camorra's predation is referenced, never staged.** Keep it at the level the Codex/Journal would record historically ("a friar known for pursuing local women forced Juli to make an impossible choice") without depicting the confrontation.
   450вҶ’   450вҶ’- **Quotations stay short and are legally/ethically safe.** Elias's famous last line and any other line from the novels used in-game should be **paraphrased** in original game dialogue rather than reproduced verbatim at length, both because these are literary works still under a publisher's edition copyright in translation and because paraphrase is simply better game-writing (it's an interactive medium, not a book excerpt viewer).
   451вҶ’   451вҶ’- **Historical figures vs. fictional figures stay clearly separated in the Codex UI** (e.g., a small "Fictional character created by Rizal" vs. "Historical figure" tag on every entry) вҖ” this single UI tag does a huge amount of the game's actual *educational* labor, since student confusion between the two is the #1 real-world misconception the game should correct.
